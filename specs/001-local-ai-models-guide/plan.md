## Summary

Create a comprehensive blog post (3,000-6,000 words) comparing open source AI models in early 2026 against frontier models, covering benchmark analysis, local model optimization (quantization, sampling, Ollama config), search/RAG augmentation tools, and benchmark literacy. The post follows the established blog voice (direct, cynical, opinionated, data-backed) and implements SEO/AEO/GEO optimization through structured frontmatter (FAQ, keyTakeaways, tldr), TLDR-first content structure, fact-dense prose with attributed sources, and proper JSON-LD schema.
