I'm excited to share my new portfolio site with you! This site showcases my work as a software developer and provides a platform for me to share my thoughts on technology and software development.

## What to Expect

On this site, you'll find:

- **Projects**: A collection of my work, from Telegram bots to web platforms
- **Blog**: Articles about software development, technology, and lessons learned
- **Experience**: My professional journey and education
- **Contact**: Ways to get in touch with me

## Built With Modern Tech

This portfolio is built with:

- **Next.js 15**: The latest version with App Router
- **TypeScript**: For type safety and better developer experience
- **React Three Fiber**: For the 3D hero scene
- **Tailwind CSS**: For styling with a Stripe-inspired design
- **Framer Motion**: For smooth animations
- **MDX**: For rich blog content
